Cookie Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026. External forms and booking tools are blocked until you actively allow them.

Necessary storage

Camp-Planner stores your external-content preference in your browser under cp-external-content-consent. This is necessary to remember whether you accepted or declined external services. The public website otherwise aims to operate without analytics or advertising cookies.

Change or withdraw your choice

You can change your preference at any time. Withdrawing consent prevents external content from loading on future page views; cookies already stored by a provider may need to be removed through your browser settings.
