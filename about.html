About the founder

Built from the floor.
Designed for the future.

Camp-Planner was created by Martin Stolpe-Thomsen — combining professional software leadership with real-world experience from organizing, teaching and supporting international sports camps.

Professional side

Software leadership. Product thinking. Operational structure.

Martin has worked professionally with product management, business development and operational execution — including experience as a former CEO of a software development company.

This gives Camp-Planner a strong foundation in how software products should be structured: clear workflows, scalable systems, usable interfaces and real operational value.

Sports event side

Kancho. Founder. Camp organizer.

Martin is the founder and Kancho of Nivå Budoklub and the Kancho of MAAS Denmark.

He travels internationally to teach, support and help run camps — giving him direct experience with the pressure, complexity and responsibility behind successful sports events.

Camp-Planner is built from that reality: live changes, check-in, instructors, participants, venues, schedules, communication and the need to keep everything moving.

“Camp-Planner is professional enough for organizations and practical enough for the people actually running the event.”
