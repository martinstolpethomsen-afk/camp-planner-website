Why we build

We know the pressure behind the perfect event.

Behind every great camp, seminar or tournament there is a team of people trying to make everything work: participants, instructors, schedules, areas, check-in, changes and communication.

Camp-Planner was created to make that work calmer, smarter and more professional — without removing the human energy that makes sport events special.

Founder & Product Vision

Martin Stolpe-Thomsen

Martin brings together experience from software development, martial arts and large event planning. He has previously worked as CEO in a software development company and has spent years building systems, structures and communities around sport.

As founder of Nivå Budoklub and Kancho of MAAS Denmark, Martin understands what happens on the floor when the schedule changes, people arrive, instructors ask questions and the organizer still needs to keep control.

CEO

Trine Kirkeby

Trine is CEO of Camp-Planner and the owner behind the company. She is a former professional handball player with a lifelong understanding of sport, performance, discipline and teamwork.

Professionally, Trine works with financial management in an international company. Privately, she runs a home with four children and a husband who always has a thousand new ideas.

She is also a board member of Nivå Budoklub and national secretary of MAAS Denmark. After her handball career, she trained fitness and later found a new passion in 3XF — kickboxing combined with self-defence.

Trine has helped organize countless camps and events over the years and brings a rare mix of structure, calm, sport experience and real-life execution to Camp-Planner.
